Getting Here

By Air

Fly to Inverness Airport (INV). Regular flights from London, Edinburgh, Bristol, Manchester, and other UK cities.

Flight to Inverness ~1.5 hrs from London
Drive to Invercharron ~50 mins, 40 miles via A9 north

From Inverness you can hire a car (Hertz, Enterprise, and Avis at the airport) or book a taxi.

By Train

LNER runs direct from London King’s Cross to Inverness via Edinburgh, Perth, and Aviemore.

London King’s Cross → Inverness ~7.5–8.5 hrs direct
Taxi or drive to Invercharron ~50 mins from Inverness

Book at lner.co.uk or thetrainline.com. You can also change at Edinburgh for ScotRail through the Cairngorms (~3.5 hrs) — a lovely route.

By Sleeper

The Caledonian Sleeper runs overnight from London Euston to Inverness.

Depart London Euston ~21:00
Arrive Inverness ~08:30 next morning
Taxi or drive to Invercharron ~50 mins

Seated, classic rooms (bunks) or club rooms (double with en suite). Lounge car for supper and breakfast. Sunday to Friday nights. Book at caledoniansleeper.com — club rooms sell out quickly.

From Inverness

All routes arrive in Inverness. The drive north is ~50 minutes on the A9, then over Struie Hill.

What to Pack

Evenings & Events

  • Smart casual for dinner unless told otherwise — jacket and smart trousers or a smart dress.
  • Northern Meeting: white tie (summer), black tie (winter).

Daytime & Outdoors

  • Warm layers. It can be brisk even in summer.
  • A waterproof coat. It will rain.
  • Wellington boots are available at the house, but bring your own if you prefer a guaranteed fit.
  • Swimming things in summer. The river is cold but good.
